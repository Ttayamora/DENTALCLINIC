Endodontics Dentistry

Not long ago, the only option available for patients that were desperately suffering from a badly damaged and decayed or injured tooth was tooth extraction. A lot of people lost their teeth unnecessarily. Today, advancements in endodontic treatments make it possible for you to save your teeth and avoid extraction. Endodontics is the branch of dentistry that specializes in treatments that focuses on your tooth roots and dental pulp.

What are the Endodontic Treatments available?

Your tooth can be saved with endodontic treatment in majority of cases. When you experience symptoms of swelling or pain, visit us immediately. Here are some of the endodontic treatments available in our clinic.

  1. Root Canal Treatment

  2. Endodontic Retreatment

  3. Apicoectomy
